About Us

S4DS at K. J. Somaiya Institute of Technology is a student-led community that explores Data Science, AI, and emerging tech. We focus on real-world projects, hands-on learning, and peer collaboration through events like our flagship hackathon, Knowcode.

Vision

To build a future-ready tech community where students innovate with impact to solve real problems and drive change across society, industry, and government.

Mission

To empower students through practical learning, mentorship, and innovation. We aim to connect minds, build meaningful projects, and share knowledge that creates lasting change.

KNOWHOW

Knowhow is a place where like minded people come together, discuss ideas and contribute to each other's growth by sharing their experiences and learning together as well as build a strong network together. Anybody with an acceptable amount of skills and/or dedication towards their field can be a part of it. Community will always be a free and open place with no or very little administrative hierarchy.
